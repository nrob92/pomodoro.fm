# Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026

Pomodoro.FM respects your privacy.

Pomodoro.FM does not require an account and does not sell your personal information. Most app data, including timer settings, theme preferences, audio preferences, and session history, is stored locally on your device.

If you enable notifications, Pomodoro.FM requests notification permission from iOS so the app can alert you when focus or break sessions end.

If you use optional custom media link features, the app may connect to third-party services to retrieve media information or playback resources. Those services may receive technical information such as your IP address as part of those requests.

If you contact support, we receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address and message content.

Pomodoro.FM does not use your information for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Data stored locally on your device remains there until you delete it in the app or remove the app from your device. Support emails are retained only as long as needed to respond and keep basic support records.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact roycerobins95@gmail.com.
